## Overview
The Planning Poker App is a real-time web application designed to facilitate agile software estimation. Teams can seamlessly collaborate by joining a shared lobby via a unique link, allowing for distributed or co-located sprint planning sessions. 

## Key Features
- **Lobby System:** Users can generate and share unique links to invite team members into a specific planning session.
- **Fibonacci Voting System:** The app uses the standard agile Fibonacci sequence (0, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21, etc.) for task estimation.
- **Skip Functionality:** A dedicated 'Skip' or '?' card is available for participants who are unable to estimate the current task.
- **Real-time Reveal:** All votes are kept hidden until the round concludes. Once all participants have voted (or skipped), the cards are revealed simultaneously.
- **Automated Calculation:** Upon revealing the cards, the system automatically calculates and displays the average score of the non-skipped votes.
- **Round Management:** A simple mechanism to reset the board and start the next estimation round.

## Target Audience
- Agile development teams (Scrum Masters, Product Owners, Developers, and QA).
- Remote and distributed teams requiring a synchronized estimation tool.
